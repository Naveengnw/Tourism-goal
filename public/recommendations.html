Strategic Recommendations for NWP Tourism

Based on the data analysis, the following recommendations are proposed to enhance the tourism sector in the North Western Province.

🔌

Improve Connectivity

Focus on "last mile" transport from main hubs (e.g., Kurunegala) to rural heritage and eco-tourism sites.

🏨

Diversify Accommodation

Incentivize development of eco-lodges and homestays in areas like Puttalam and near Wilpattu to cater to nature tourists.

🌙

Develop the Night Economy

Promote evening cultural shows and lighted heritage sites in urban centers to extend tourist engagement past daytime hours.

🤝

Boost Community Participation

Create programs that train and certify local guides and craftspeople, ensuring tourism revenue directly benefits communities.
